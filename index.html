Introducing the Seroque AI Assistant

Seroque will feature a built-in AI assistant designed to support you throughout your academic journey. This intelligent companion can help you manage your workload, optimize your routines, and provide personalized guidance whenever you need it.

  • Schedule Creation: Automatically generate and adjust study, research, and rest schedules based on your goals, deadlines, and preferred work patterns.
  • Homework Help: Get explanations, brainstorming support, and step-by-step guidance for assignments, problem sets, and research questions across disciplines.
  • Task Prioritization: Let the AI help you break down complex projects, set priorities, and track progress so nothing falls through the cracks.
  • Wellness Reminders: Receive gentle nudges to take breaks, hydrate, meditate, or get some fresh air—helping you maintain balance and avoid burnout.
  • Resource Recommendations: Instantly find relevant articles, papers, and tools tailored to your field and current project.
  • Motivation & Accountability: The assistant can check in on your goals, celebrate your wins, and help you stay on track with positive reinforcement.
  • Customizable Support: Whether you need daily planning, last-minute deadline triage, or just someone to talk through your ideas, the AI adapts to your unique needs and preferences.

With the Seroque AI assistant, you’ll have a tireless, knowledgeable partner—empowering you to work smarter, reduce stress, and make the most of your academic experience.
